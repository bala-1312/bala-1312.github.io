Musings

Concerning Bachelor’s2025 · 10

I liked bunking mandhir; I also liked going there.
I liked the haircut the barber gave; even more when it was my friend.
I liked standing in queue; also when I slipped past it.
I liked being served with disparity; also when treated like royalty (by friends, of course).
I liked walking; appreciated it when I couldn't.
I liked when I threw a bottle at my friend, also when he forgot.
I liked reading; also when I understood.
I liked my room without roommates; also when they were.
I liked talks; small, big, coded, misinterpreted.
Also silence.
I liked when I didn't care; also when I did.
I liked when I slipped; also at its inevitability.
I liked it all; though not always while it was happening.
